Practice Times and Open Gym Hours

September 4, 2019

Welcome back! This year, practices will be Sundays 1-3pm at North Campus Recreational Building (NCRB). If you are looking for times to play outside of practice, open gym is a perfect way to meet new people and hit. Open gym hours are at NCRB on Mondays 6-11pm, Wednesdays 6-11pm, Fridays 6-10pm, and Saturdays 4-10pm. There are also open gym hours at Central Campus Recreational Building (CCRB) on Tuesdays 7-11pm, Thursdays 7-11pm, Saturdays 8-12pm, Sundays 8-12pm, Sundays 4-11pm, and Monday-through-Friday 12-3pm.
