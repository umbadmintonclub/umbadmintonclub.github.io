About the Club

Welcome! The Michigan Badminton Club is a non-exclusive community that aims to provide and build a place for badminton enthusiasts. We are open to all students and faculties on campus regardless of background or skill level. Members will have the opportunity to learn the basics, improve their skills, and compete against players across the Midwest. We host weekly practices, team bonding social events, and even tournaments for anyone and everyone. Be prepared to meet new people, travel, and experience real Badminton. We can't wait to see you there!
